Program Overview

The diploma prepares you to write the Entry-to-Practice Exam administered by the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC) to obtain the Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) designation. As a registered Immigration Consultant, you are able to work and practice across Canada as well as in other Countries
